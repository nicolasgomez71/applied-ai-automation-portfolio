# Six-Month Technical Roadmap

## Month 1
Python basics, Git/GitHub, terminal, SQL basics and technical documentation.

## Month 2
REST APIs, JSON, Postman or Hoppscotch, troubleshooting, logs and SaaS support workflows.

## Month 3
Workflow automation with n8n, Make, Zapier or Power Automate. Webhooks, forms, email, spreadsheets and CRM-style processes.

## Month 4
Generative AI APIs, prompt design, basic RAG, embeddings, vector databases and response evaluation.

## Month 5
Power BI or reporting, QA/API testing, dashboards, operational analysis and applied SQL.

## Month 6
Portfolio polishing, LinkedIn, CV, interview preparation, job applications and technical demos.
